# Learnings from 2026-02-03

## Biochoric
- **Isochoric Supercooling**: -4°C is optimal for rat hearts (reduced edema). Lower temps (-6°C/-8°C) cause pressure damage (>60MPa).
- **HECPA**: High Entropy Solutions. >2 solutes, entropy of mixing >= 7 J/molK. Enables ice-free storage in the -20°C to -80°C range.

## InterScience
- **E2**: Next-gen IRE. Uses modulated exponential decay pulses + electrolysis. Faster, safer, no muscle contraction.
- **Product**: QuickPulse generator.
